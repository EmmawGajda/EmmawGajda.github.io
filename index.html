Emma Woon Gajda


Driven. Supportive. Curious.



Emma is a junior at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, studying English in the College of Humanities and Fine Arts. With a concentration in Writing, Rhetoric, and Literacy, she’s interested in the ways language shapes thinking, communities, and stories.



She believes that a love of reading and learning starts with a book—and someone willing to sit down and share it with you.



As a tutor for K–2 students, Emma supports early literacy by helping children find joy and confidence in reading.


Whether she’s working through tricky phonics or diving into a favorite picture book, she’s all about creating positive, lasting connections with language.


Emma currently lives in Amherst, Massachusetts, and, when she’s not immersed in a book or studying, enjoys spending time with her family, her four dogs, and working as a hand at the UMass Hadley Farm.
