My Dogs!

Just like everyone else who owns dogs... I believe that mine are the best.

My first dog is Ginger. She is an aging chocolate lab who I got on my eigth birthday, and who I believe is a literal angel sent from above.
She is loyal, loving, sweet, and now that she's a bit older, very calm. She is a true pal.

My second dog, and this order is based on when I got them, is Sadie. She is a very quiet, very shy, lab/shiba mix.
She loves to sit and watch the world from a quiet corner, but she is also a huge love bug.

My family and I believe she had a rough go of it when she was a puppy, and that is why she is so jumpy.
However, she's also a bit deaf and her breed (shiba inu's) are known for being more sentinel than party animal.

My two final dogs, Joy a four-year-old grumpy black lab/corgi mix, and Roo, a one-year-old pitbull/lab mix, are the babies.
They are sweet and fun, though Joy is more cat than dog, and I absolutely adore them.
